How do installment payments work?
We simplify your payments by automatically withdrawing 22 equal installments from your business bank account. Installment payments will begin 5 business days after we deposit your cash request. We ask your bank to deduct installment payments during business days only. So you have a little over a month to pay back the debt considering we ask the bank not to deduct installments during weekends and holidays.

Tax ID or EIN, business zip code, business bank account, e-mail or phone number associated with your business bank account.

What account do you deposit my cash request and withdraw payments form?
During the application process we ask you to link your business bank account, which we use, as your primary payment account. Your primary payment account is the account we deposit your cash requests and withdraw installment payments from.
How is my payment amount determined?
The daily amount deducted will be made up of your requested cash amount plus a one-time 4.99% service charge, divided by 22 installments.
When do payments post to my business bank account?
Installment payments will begin 5 business days after we deposit your cash request. We ask your bank to deduct installment payments during business days only.
What fees are charged for a cash request?
A one-time 4.99% service charge shall be added to the cash request amount to cover money transferring and order processing fees.
What if I can’t find my bank in the list of banks provided?
We use 3rd party data provider Plaid to securely connect to your financial institution. If you cannot find your bank in the list of banks provided it’s simply because Plaid does not support connectivity to your bank and as such we will not be able to extend you a cash request until Plaid provides us the capability to connect to your bank. To request for your bank to be added to Plaid list of banks contact us at help@motechnologiesusa.com.
How long does it take to be approved?
The application and approval process only takes a few minutes.
How do you determine how much cash I can request?
We use modern technology to review the financial performance of your business. We then crunch some numbers and share the maximum cash amount we can provide you.
If I own multiple businesses, can I open multiple MO accounts?
If you own multiple businesses, you can open multiple MO accounts as long as each business has its own unique business bank account. You cannot link the same business bank account to multiple MO accounts.
What do you do with my business bank login credentials?
We use a 3rd party data provider Plaid to securely connect to your financial institution. We never store your login credentials and only maintains the account connection through Plaid.
